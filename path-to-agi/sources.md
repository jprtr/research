## Source Quality Policy

Per the Vanguard Research Directive:
- T1 sources are the foundation for all factual claims about model capabilities and AGI trajectory
- T2 sources (essays, papers, interviews) provide expert analysis and context
- T3 sources are discovery tools for detecting emerging signals, not establishing capabilities
- Every URL must be opened and validated before citation
- Unavailable sources are marked as "not captured" rather than represented as real
- Benchmark claims must cite the benchmark owner, not third-party leaderboards or summaries
